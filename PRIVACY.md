# Privacy Policy for Meeting Intelligence

**Last updated: February 3, 2026**

## Overview
Meeting Intelligence is designed with privacy in mind. Your meeting data stays on your device.

## Data Collection
- **Audio/Transcription:** All audio processing and transcription happens locally on your Mac using the Whisper model. No audio is sent to external servers.
- **AI Analysis:** When you use AI analysis features, your transcribed text is sent to your chosen AI provider (OpenAI, Anthropic/Claude, or Ollama). If you use Ollama, all processing stays local.
- **No Personal Data Collection:** We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

## Third-Party Services
If you choose to use OpenAI or Claude for analysis, your transcript text is sent to their APIs. Please review their privacy policies:
- OpenAI: https://openai.com/privacy
- Anthropic: https://www.anthropic.com/privacy

## Data Storage
All meeting data (transcripts, summaries) is stored locally on your Mac. We have no access to your data.

## Contact
For privacy questions, open an issue at: https://github.com/mcosmi-design/MeetingIntelligence-Support
