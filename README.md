## About

## FAQ

**Q: What AI providers are supported?**
A: Meeting Intelligence supports OpenAI, Claude (Anthropic), and Ollama (local/offline).

**Q: Is my data private?**
A: Yes. All transcription happens locally on your Mac. Meeting data is only sent to your chosen AI provider for analysis.
